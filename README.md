Welcome to Through The Darkness, a 2D game that takes you on a thrilling adventure through a dangerous cityscape, underground tunnels, and a treacherous maze. Immerse yourself in the story of a heroic individual who risks everything to for justice. Will you help them evade capture and uncover the truth?

# Gameplay

In Through The Darkness, you assume the role of the courageous whistleblower who has decided to leak classified information about a dangerous chemical plant for the greater good of humanity. The game unfolds in four gripping stages:

City Escape: Flee the pursuing enemies in your trusty car as you navigate through the busy highway. Avoid obstacles and enemy vehicles while staying ahead of the patrol.

Dungeon Descend: Seek refuge in the depths of the city, where you must navigate a network of secret tunnels in darkness to stay hidden from the relentless enemies. Stay vigilant, as danger lurks around every corner.

Maze: Get lost in maze filled with dead-ends, and enemies.

# Controls
Move left: A
Move right: D
Candle light: Spacebar

Engaging storyline that unfolds through four challenging stages.

Dynamic obstacles, enemy AI, and puzzle-solving elements.
Multiple difficulty levels to suit players of all skill levels.
